MediNeti Outreach System

NABH Renewal Campaign Management Portal

Internal Use Only

⚠ Internal MediNeti Tool

This application is intended exclusively for MediNeti Healthcare Solutions team members. It is used for importing hospital contact databases and sending NABH renewal outreach emails.

📤 Upload Contacts

Upload hospital contact records using an Excel (.xlsx/.xls) or CSV file. Records will be validated and inserted into the database.

📧 Send Email Campaign

Send NABH renewal outreach emails to all contacts where already_sent = FALSE.

🧪 Test Email

Send a test email using the contact configured with hcos_id = 0.

🔄 Reset Sent Status

Mark contacts whose email was sent more than X days ago as unsent, so they are eligible for the next campaign.

✉️ Send Individual Email

Send a NABH renewal outreach email to a single hospital by entering the hospital name and recipient email address.
